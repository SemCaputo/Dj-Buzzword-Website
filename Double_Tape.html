Double Tape Drums

Double Tape Drums (DTD) is our new series focusing on rich and interesting drum sounds, based on our love of recording to analogue tape. This edition blends analogue synthesizer (mainly the Moog DFAM) drum sounds recorded to Tascam Porta 1 and Tascam 144 cassette machines, with chops takenfrom our vast catalogue of Reel to Reel drum break recording sessions. The result being 188 punchy one shots including:

  • 75 Kicks
  • 72 Snares, Claps & Rimshots
  • 16 Hihats
  • 14 Toms
  • 11 FX
