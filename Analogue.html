Analog Effects & Textures

The British Music Library - Analogue Effects & Textures gives a nod to the old school sound designers manipulating tape machines, splicing tape and utilizing modular analogue synthesizers to create otherworldly sounds. Authenticity is important to us so we used the right tools: Analogue synthesizers, vocals and percussion, seasoned with tape echo and spring reverb tracked through a vintage British console to 1/2" tape create sounds to take you to another place. Over 1.7Gb of content featuring sound effects, textures for building upon or filling in space and percussive sounds.
